# Specialist development profile

- Apply Phoxia-grade architecture, security, testing, documentation, operations and release discipline.
- Do not assume the project is open source or that third parties receive redistribution or commercial rights.
- Establish the legal owner, confidentiality, source visibility, contribution policy and distribution model before generating licensing files.
- Distinguish proprietary, internal, client-owned, source-available, dual-licensed and open-source models accurately.
- Never label a commercial-use restriction as open source.
- Treat generated license text as a reviewable draft, not legal advice.
- Keep employer and client material within its authorized boundary.
- Do not commit, push, publish, deploy, access production or migrate data without approval.
