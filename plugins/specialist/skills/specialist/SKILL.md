# Specialist workflow

Read repository instructions and `.phoxia/project.yaml` when present.

Before changing licensing or ownership files, establish:

1. legal owner and authorized approver;
2. source visibility and confidentiality boundary;
3. who may copy, modify, redistribute, commercialize or offer the software as a service;
4. derivative-work and contribution policy;
5. whether reusable pre-existing components remain with their original owner;
6. whether the intended model is proprietary, internal, client-owned, source-available, dual-licensed or open source.

Never describe a field-of-use or commercial restriction as open source. Generate drafts with a legal-review notice. Preserve existing accepted contracts and license history.
