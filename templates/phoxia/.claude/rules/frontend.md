# Phoxia interface

- Galaxy and graph views require table or outline alternatives.
- Distinguish live, historical and comparison states.
- Support keyboard navigation, screen readers and visible focus.
- Do not rely on color alone.
- Design loading, empty, degraded, error and denied states.
- Use `Phoxia • Page` for page titles.
- Avoid em dashes in normal product copy.
