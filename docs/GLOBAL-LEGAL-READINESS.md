# Global legal readiness

This is an engineering checklist, not legal advice.

Identify controllers, processors, markets, personal data, purposes, retention, deletion, security, rights, incidents, subprocessors and international transfers.

When minors may use the service:

- make their best interests a design requirement;
- use high-privacy defaults;
- minimize profile and location collection;
- keep official communication visible and moderated;
- define age assurance before restricted collection;
- avoid behavioral ads to children;
- provide guardian flows when required.

For contextual and behavioral advertising:

- label ads clearly;
- separate ads from product recommendations;
- record consent and purpose;
- offer preference, hide and report controls;
- do not target sensitive categories;
- use contextual advertising when age is unknown or the user is a child.

For passkeys, OTP, authenticator apps, device approval and number matching, bind approval to context, expire challenges, prevent replay and avoid storing biometric templates.

Draft and review Terms and Privacy before a public frontend collects accounts, analytics, ads, payments or personal data.
