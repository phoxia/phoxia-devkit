# Community Code of Conduct

We want a community where people can learn, contribute and disagree safely.

Expected behavior:

- patience with beginners;
- criticism focused on the work;
- respectful communication;
- protection of private information;
- accessible explanations;
- good-faith collaboration;
- special care when interacting with minors.

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or threats;
- discrimination;
- sexualized conduct toward community members;
- publishing private information;
- pressuring minors for private contact;
- impersonation;
- sabotage;
- repeated hostility after moderation.

Official communication with minors should remain visible, moderated and limited to what the project requires.

Maintainers may issue a correction, warning, temporary restriction or permanent ban.

Reports should be sent privately to the lead maintainer identified in `GOVERNANCE.md`.
